About Me

My experience as a personal and group fitness trainer built a foundation of consistency and commitment. It also taught me to be comfortable and effective with communicating new concepts to customers and teammates. Currently I am in a fourteen week program to become a software developer. A year ago I was researching to find a career I could grow in. My brother, a computer scientist, knew I enjoyed puzzles and problem solving. He suggested a coding bootcamp to me and I have been hooked since day one of classes at WeCanCodeIt. I adapted from building targeted workouts, to building user focused full stack software. I cannot wait to see where this journey takes me.

Instructions

Welcome to the donut clicking game!

  • Click the donut to start making donuts.
  • Use your donuts to research new sprinkle colors. Each new sprinkle color multiplies your potential donuts by 2.2!
  • Use your donuts to hire bakers. Each baker will make one donut per second for you!
  • Use your donuts to upgrade your bakery. Each bakery upgrade gives you space to hire a new baker!
  • Get out there and get clicking. There are donuts to make!
